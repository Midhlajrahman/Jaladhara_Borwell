About Us

Welcome to Jaladhara Borewells - Your Water Solution Experts!

Jaladhara Borewells is a government-registered borewell contractor with over 10 years of invaluable experience. Specializing in 5, 6, and 7-inch tube well construction, we provide permanent drinking water solutions through precision computerized surveying. Located on Perinthalmanna Road, Koppam, with a branch in Perinthalmanna, we offer a comprehensive range of services, including well drilling, re-boring, borewell cleaning, computerized borewell pointing, pump set fitting, and repairing. Our commitment to quality products, cost-effective solutions, and a decade of service excellence makes us your go-to partner for reliable water solutions.
